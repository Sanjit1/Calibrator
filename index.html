Thermistor Calculator

Introduction

Lets get into the details of Thermistors, and Thermistor Calibration. A thermistor is a resistor, whose resistance changes, based on the temperature. We will be working with NTC thermistors. NTC thermistors are namded after the resistance they provide at 25 °C. So a 10K thermistor should read about 10K resistance at 25°C.
